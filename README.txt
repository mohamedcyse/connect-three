#Connect three is a game that is similar to tic tac toe.
-Using red and yellow coins instead of Xs and Os.
-Two players red and yellow who take turns marking the spaces in a 3x3 grid.
-The player who succeeds in placing three of their marks in a horizontal, vertical or
diagonal row wins the game.


#GridLayout is used to contain imageviews representing the coins.

#multidimensional array is used to obtain the possible winning positions.

#Flags are used to determine whether the current player is red or yellow and to determine
whether the game is over or not.
